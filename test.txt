The volume of the explanatory note is 64 pages. Number of pictures - 10. Number of tables - 5. Number of sources - 28. The object of study is the social network. The subject of the study is to informational influence on the social network. The study was realized with the help of the following research methods: treatment of the theoretical literature on the subject of the existing models of influence in social networks, an analysis of their strengths, weaknesses and limits of application. Has been investigated and analyzed the influence of a Markov model of information in social networks. In her opinion formation and dynamics of agents are described using the apparatus of Markov chains, and the opinions of agents are calculated using the graph influences. Identified the strengths and weaknesses of this model, the application limits are possible generalizations of the model, and investigated the possibility of using this model in solving the problems of information management and information warfare.
